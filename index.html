About Me

Hi! I am a Research Engineer in Extreme Classification group at Microsoft Research (MSR) under the guidance of Dr. Manik Varma. My primary focus involves developing machine learning algorithms for large-scale industrial applications in search and recommendation. I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Ropar in 2021, where I was honored with the Director’s Gold Medal for my overall performance. During my UG, I worked closely with Dr. Abhinav Dhall exploring interpretable deep learning architectures with a particular emphasis on their applications in healthcare.
