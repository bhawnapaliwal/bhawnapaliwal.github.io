About Me

Hi! I am a Research Engineer in Extreme Classification group at Microsoft Research (MSR) under the guidance of Dr. Manik Varma. My primary focus involves developing scalable machine learning algorithms for applications in search and recommendation. I completed my undergraduate studies majoring in Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Ropar in 2021, where I was honored with the Director’s Gold Medal for my overall performance. My research interests lie in the scalability and robustness of machine learning models, encompassing efficient architectures, training algorithms, and ensuring robustness across distribution shifts, tasks, modalities, and beyond. If you are interested in machine learning, research, or building solutions, I am sure we will have plenty to chat about :)
